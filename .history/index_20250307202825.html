Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website development services.

How long will it take to build my website?

Most projects are completed within 3-4 weeks from start to finish. The Professional and Premium packages may take slightly longer due to the additional features. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the website?

Not at all! We design our websites with user-friendly content management systems that make it easy to update content, add products, and manage bookings. We also provide comprehensive training and documentation to help you get comfortable with your new website.

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive, meaning they automatically adjust to look great and function perfectly on any device - smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. We offer flexible payment plans with 50% due upfront and the remaining 50% upon project completion.

What if I need changes after the website launches?

All packages include 30 days of free support after launch. For ongoing support and maintenance, we recommend our monthly maintenance plan, which includes regular updates, security monitoring, and content changes as needed.
